Our café & bakery

Come & meet us on the corner

We're a small café with a big oven — started by two friends who couldn't find a proper breakfast in the neighbourhood, so we built one. The ovens downstairs are on at 4am, so the croissants are warm by the time you're awake. Plants in the window, jazz on the speakers, jars of house marmalade on every table.
